This is a list of computer programs that are predominantly used for molecular mechanics calculations.

  • GPU – GPU accelerated
  • I – Has interface
  • Imp – Implicit water
  • MC – Monte Carlo
  • MD – Molecular dynamics
  • Min – Optimization
  • QM – Quantum mechanics
  • REM – Replica exchange method
